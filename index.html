How It Works

For Brands

For Creators

Signup As a Brand

Join Collabr as a brand and complete your profile. Provide all necessary details to get started.

Find Creators or Launch Campaign

Find thousands of creators and select as per your need. Launch your campaign and get creators quotation.

Do Collab, Pay Securely!

Do collaboration with your selected creators. We hold your payment until the work is completed and you accept it.

Signup As a Creator

Join Collabr as a creator and complete your profile. No upfront or hidden fees. Only 10% platform fees on successful collaboration.

Get Found & Connect

Once your profile will be live, brand can search your profile and connect through Collabr directly without showing your contact details.

Do Collab, Get Paid!

Chat with brand, do the assigned task and get paid securely with 100% payment protection. Within 48 hours, get your earned amount in you back account.
